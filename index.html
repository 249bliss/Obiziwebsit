Our Foundation

Unity that runs deep,
Vision that soars high

Ekenobizi is a community of individuals committed to autonomous growth for all. A bonded, indigenous community established and governed by its own people. This autonomous community has a rich history spanning decades of communal unity, cultural preservation, and progressive development.

They are also a mixture of a diverse culture of people who speak different languages yet share the same vision of a peaceful, prosperous community where every member thrives.
